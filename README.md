# 🍽️ Meal Mood Picker

> *Not sure what to eat? Let your mood decide.*

A simple interactive web app that suggests a meal based on the **time of day** and your **current mood** — built with vanilla JavaScript as a school project.

---

## ✨ How it works

Select the time of day and how you're feeling, hit the button — and get a personalized meal suggestion with ingredients and a step-by-step recipe.

---

## 🕐 Time of day options

| Time | 🇵🇱 |
|------|-----|
| Morning | Rano |
| Noon | Południe |
| Night | Noc |

## 😶 Mood options

| Mood | 🇵🇱 |
|------|-----|
| Sad | Smutny |
| Normal | Normalny |
| Happy | Wesoły |

---

## 🍳 Meals included

| Time | Sad | Normal | Happy |
|------|-----|--------|-------|
| 🌅 Morning | Chocolate Oatmeal | Ham & Cheese Toast | Pancakes |
| ☀️ Noon | Chicken Salad | Homemade Burger | Pasta with Egg |
| 🌙 Night | Butter Scrambled Eggs | Hot Sausages | French Toast |
